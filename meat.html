Creamy Lemon Chicken Serves: 4 Hands-on time: 15 minutes Total time: 40 minutes You’ll Need: 1 Knorr Chicken Stock Pot 1 tbsp oil 600g skinless, boneless chicken breast 1 tsp dried oregano or dried thyme 1 lemon, sliced 80ml crème fraîche 1 tsp honey Dissolve the Knorr Chicken Stock Pot in 250ml of boiling water. Heat the oil in a large frying pan, and fry the chicken until lightly browned, in batches if necessary. Add the garlic and fry for 1 more minute. Add the stock, herbs and lemon. Simmer for 15-20 minutes or until chicken is cooked through. Remove the chicken and keep warm. Whisk the crème fraîche and honey into the sauce. Boil and reduce until slightly thickened. Return the chicken to the pan and reheat. Serve with cooked rice or pasta, and green vegetables.
